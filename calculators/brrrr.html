Understanding the BRRRR Strategy

What is BRRRR?

BRRRR stands for Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. It's a real estate investment strategy that allows investors to recycle their capital and scale their portfolio faster than traditional buy-and-hold methods.

  • • Buy: Purchase property below market value
  • • Rehab: Renovate to increase value
  • • Rent: Generate positive cash flow
  • • Refinance: Pull out invested capital
  • • Repeat: Use recycled cash for next property

Key Success Factors

  • • Buy Right: Purchase significantly below ARV
  • • Accurate ARV: Conservative after-repair value estimates
  • • Budget Control: Stick to renovation budget
  • • Market Rent: Realistic rental income projections
  • • Financing: Secure favorable refinance terms
  • • Exit Strategy: Plan for different market conditions

BRRRR Strategy Tips

Buy Smart

  • • Research locations
  • • Analyze comps
  • • Negotiate effectively
  • • Account for all costs

Rehab Right

  • • Budget accurately
  • • Quality contractors
  • • Timeline management
  • • Value-add improvements

Rent & Refinance

  • • Market rent research
  • • Quality tenants
  • • Appraisal timing
  • • Lender relationships

Repeat Success

  • • Scale systematically
  • • Track performance
  • • Reinvest proceeds
  • • Exit strategy planning

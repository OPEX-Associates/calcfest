📊 Investment Guidelines

Good Investment Indicators:

  • • Positive cash flow from day 1
  • • Cap rate above 8-10%
  • • 1% rule (rent ≥ 1% of purchase price)
  • • Cash-on-cash return above 8-12%

Common Expense Rules:

  • • Vacancy: 5-10% of gross rent
  • • Maintenance: 5-10% of gross rent
  • • Property management: 8-12% of gross rent
  • • Property taxes: Varies by location

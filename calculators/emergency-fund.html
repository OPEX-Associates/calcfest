Emergency Fund Guidelines

Standard Recommendations

3-6 Months

Standard recommendation for stable employment and good health insurance coverage.

6-12 Months

For variable income, self-employment, or specialized careers with longer job search times.

12+ Months

For high-risk situations, major health concerns, or very specialized professions.

What Qualifies as Emergency

Job loss or income reduction
Major medical expenses
Major home or car repairs
Family emergencies
NOT for vacations or purchases

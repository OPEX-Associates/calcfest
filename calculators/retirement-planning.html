💡 Retirement Planning Tips

Start Early & Be Consistent:

  • • Time is your greatest asset for compound growth
  • • Automate savings to build consistent habits
  • • Increase contributions with salary raises
  • • Don't try to time the market

Diversify & Plan for Healthcare:

  • • Mix of stocks, bonds, and other assets
  • • Consider international diversification
  • • Plan for rising healthcare costs
  • • Consider long-term care insurance

⚠️ Common Retirement Mistakes

Planning Errors:

  • • Underestimating retirement expenses
  • • Not accounting for inflation
  • • Retiring with debt
  • • No backup plan for market downturns

Investment Mistakes:

  • • Being too conservative or too aggressive
  • • Not rebalancing portfolios
  • • Panic selling during market drops
  • • Ignoring fees and expenses

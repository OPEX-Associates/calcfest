Understanding Down Payments & PMI

Down Payment Basics

  • 20% = No PMI: Putting down 20% or more eliminates the need for PMI
  • Lower rates: Higher down payments often qualify for better interest rates
  • Instant equity: Your down payment becomes immediate home equity
  • Lower payments: Larger down payment means smaller loan and lower monthly payments

PMI Explained

  • Protects lender: PMI protects the lender if you default on the loan
  • Required <20%: Mandatory when down payment is less than 20%
  • Auto removal: Automatically cancelled when you reach 78% LTV
  • Request removal: Can request removal at 80% LTV with appraisal

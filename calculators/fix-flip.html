Fix & Flip Investment Guide

Key Success Factors

  • 70% Rule: Purchase price + renovation costs ≤ 70% of ARV
  • Accurate ARV: Use recent comparable sales in the area
  • Contingency Buffer: Plan for 15-20% cost overruns
  • Speed is Key: Minimize holding time to reduce costs

Common Pitfalls

  • Over-Improving: Don't renovate beyond neighborhood standards
  • Underestimating Costs: Hidden issues can blow budgets
  • Wrong Location: Choose areas with strong buyer demand
  • Extended Timeline: Holding costs can eliminate profits

15-Year vs 30-Year Mortgage: Which is Right for You?

15-Year Mortgage Advantages

  • Massive interest savings: Often $100k-200k+ less in total interest
  • Faster equity building: Own your home outright in half the time
  • Lower interest rates: Typically 0.25-0.75% lower than 30-year
  • Forced savings: Builds wealth through required higher payments

30-Year Mortgage Advantages

  • Lower monthly payments: More affordable monthly cash flow
  • Investment opportunities: Invest difference for potentially higher returns
  • Financial flexibility: More room for emergencies and life changes
  • Tax advantages: Longer period of mortgage interest deductions
